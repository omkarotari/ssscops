Principal's Message

Dear Students,

In 1984,the face of quality education smiled in Konkan region. The Sahyadri Shikshan Sanstha’s COLLEGE OF PHARMACY(POLY),Sawarde was established in the pleasant & peaceful environs of Konkan on Goa-Mumbai Highway.

Today the college symbolizes the meaning of quality education & professional achievements. The campus boasts of every possible amenity. Apart from an imposing main building, the college has spacious well ventilated furnished classrooms well equipped laborities,hostel,indoor stadium,auditorium,audio-visual room,etc.in short every possible accessory to ensure the highest degree of quality education.

-Mr. Sanjay Gulabrao Desai(Principal)
